# Access

## Coming to Rennes
**Nearby Airports:**
-   [Rennes Airport (RNS)](https://www.rennes.aeroport.fr/en#)
-   [Paris Charles de Gaule ](https://www.parisaeroport.fr/en) and then connexion using high-speed train (TGV) from CDG to Rennes

**By Train:**
- Regular high-speed train (TGV) from Paris Montparnasse to Rennes

## Coming to the Venue
- By bus:
    - Line C4 or C6, stop at "Tournebride"
- By Subway:
    - Line B, stop at "Beaulieu Université"
- By car:
    - Visitor parking is available in the 600th Anniversary Hall parking garage.

If you are coming from the train station, you can:
- Take subway line B from "Gares" to "Beaulieu Université
- Take the subway line A from "Gares" to "République", then take bus C4 or C6
